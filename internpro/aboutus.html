>>Mission

Our main mission is to enable students find the perfectly matched course and college in india.It will help the students to fulfill their aspirations by setting up realistic goals.It not only by providing them their dream job but also by providing them a better future with job satisfaction.

>>What do we do??

The platform brings together experts,counsellors and educators to steer the career road map for students under parental guidance in a systematic,experiential and engaging manner.The platform inspires young learners to engage without any fear of judgement to uncover the potential of learners insted of traditionally unengaging psychometric analysis. Dream Designer digs deeper into understanding every student's unique natural potential,which is identified using the three major factors-- personality , career interest, and aptitude.It enables us to make appropriate career recommendations that are best suited for the student.

>>Why do we need to design our future

To explore and plan for future career endevors based on their individual interests,skills and values.Participation in Dream Designer enhances linkage of academic and career experiences and thus,improves career preparation and management.

>>Steps to be followed


1.DREAM IT

Firstly,We make the learners to be clear with their dream or goal.And we guide them to dream of the possibilities for yourself , yourself your family and for others.


2.BELIEVE IT

Your dream needs to be big .It needs to be something that is beyond your capabilities.But it also must be believable.Though it is a big dream if certain things take place, or if you work hard enough,it can be still be done.But we should not loose our hope.


3. TELL IT

One reason many dreams never go anywhere is because the dreamer keeps it all to himself. The one who wants to achieve their dream must tell that dream to many people.Because as we continually ssay it,we begin to believe it more and more.When we told others,it spurs us on to actually doing it so we don't lock foolish.


4.PLAN IT .

Every dream must have a plan .There is quoto "Get what you plan"is so true.Your dream won't just happen you plan on a regular basis and plan out the strategy for achieving the dream.Set a time table for accomplishing each task on your 'dream plan'.


5.WORK IT .

Successful are usually the hardest workers.There is an equation your short-term tasks,multiplied by time, equal to your long-term accomplishments.If you work on it each day, eventually you will achieve your dream.


6.ENJOY IT.

When you have reached your goal and you are living your dream,be sure to enjoy it.Thank youself and give a huge reward when you get there. Be gracious and generous.
